How to Empathize with Users

Oh yes! 🎨 You’ve now reached the part of Figma where the real design magic happens — Components, Styles, and Assets 💫

These are what make your design clean, reusable, and consistent — basically, they turn you from a button-dragging mortal 😅 into a design wizard 🧙‍♀️.

Let’s break it down in a simple, funny, and memorable way 👇

🧱 1️⃣ COMPONENTS — “The Reusable Lego Blocks”

💬 What They Are:
Components are like master copies of a design element that you can reuse anywhere.

Imagine you design one “Login” button 🔘 — you can make it a Component, and then use it on every page of your app. If you change the color of the main (master) button — boom! all copies update automatically ⚡

🧠 Example:

  • You design a blue “Sign Up” button.
  • You turn it into a Component.
  • You place it on 10 pages.
  • Client says: “Can we make it green instead?” 🟩
  • You change the main Component → all 10 buttons turn green! 💚

🎮 Funny Analogy: Components are like Lego pieces — build once, reuse everywhere. Change one block, and all your castles update — no rebuilding required 🏰

💡 Common Components:

  • ✅ Buttons
  • ✅ Navigation bars
  • ✅ Cards
  • ✅ Icons
  • ✅ Input fields

🧠 Bonus: Variants
Variants are different versions of the same component — like:

  • Button (default)
  • Button (hover)
  • Button (clicked)
  • Button (disabled)

All these states are grouped into one Component Set — so your design stays tidy and interactive 🎮

🎨 2️⃣ STYLES — “The Fashion Sense of Your Design”

💬 What They Are:
Styles define how your design looks — like colors, text fonts, or shadows.

Instead of picking colors manually each time (and ending up with seven types of blue 🫠), you create Styles once and reuse them everywhere.

🧠 Types of Styles in Figma:

Style Type What It Controls Example
🎨 Color StylesColors for text, buttons, backgrounds“Primary Blue,” “Error Red”
🅰️ Text StylesFont, size, weight, spacing“Heading 1,” “Body Text”
🌈 Effect StylesShadows, blurs, glowCard shadows, soft drop shadows
🧱 Grid StylesLayout grids for consistency12-column layout, 8-pixel spacing grid

💬 Example:

You define a Text Style called “Heading 1” — bold, 32px, black. Whenever you apply it, all headings stay consistent. Change “Heading 1” to blue → every heading updates too 💙 (You’re basically controlling the design universe now 😎).

😂 Funny Way to Remember: Styles are like your wardrobe — you don’t buy new clothes every day, you just reuse the ones that look good on you 👗👕

📦 3️⃣ ASSETS — “Your Design’s Treasure Box”

💬 What They Are:
The Assets Panel in Figma is your library of reusable elements — Components, Styles, and shared design files all live there 🗃️

It’s like your personal design backpack 🎒 — full of everything you need to build faster.

🧠 Example:

  • Buttons (from Components)
  • Color styles (from Styles)
  • Logos, icons, or illustrations

You can just drag and drop these into your project — no need to reinvent the wheel every time 🔄

🧩 Bonus Tip: In team projects, everyone uses the same Assets — so your app’s “Buy Now” button doesn’t turn into five different species of buttons 🐸🐍🦋

💪 Why These Three Are a Power Trio:

Feature What It Does Funny Analogy
ComponentsReusable design blocksLego pieces 🧱
StylesConsistent visual rulesWardrobe 👗
AssetsLibrary of all elementsTreasure box 🗃️

Together, they make your workflow faster, cleaner, and mistake-proof 💥

🎧 Real-Life Example:

Imagine you’re designing the Spotify app 🎵:

  • Components: Play button, song card, progress bar
  • Styles: Spotify green, fonts, shadows
  • Assets: All reusable items ready in your panel

So when you start a new page, you just drag and drop — like building with design Lego bricks 🧩🎶

🧠 Quick Recap

Concept Description Benefit
ComponentsReusable UI elementsSave time, stay consistent
StylesVisual settings (colors, text, effects)Make global changes easily
AssetsLibrary for Components & StylesStay organized and fast

💬 In Short:

Components are what you reuse.
Styles are how things look.
Assets are where you find them.

Together, they make your design system magical, consistent, and unstoppable 🪄✨
