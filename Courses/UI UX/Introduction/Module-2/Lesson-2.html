Ideation Techniques

Awesome! You’re diving into Ideation!

You’re entering one of the most fun and creative stages of design — Ideation!

This is where designers stop worrying about problems and start saying: “Okay, what cool ways can we solve this?”

Let’s make this super easy, visual, and fun — with stories, analogies, and simple examples that stick in your head forever 😄

What Is Ideation?

Ideation = “Idea Generation.”
It’s when designers think of as many creative solutions as possible for a problem — no judging, no limits, just pure imagination.

The goal is to explore every possible idea, from logical to crazy — because sometimes the crazy ones turn out to be brilliant 😎

Simple Definition:
Ideation is the creative process of generating, developing, and communicating new ideas to solve a design problem.

Real-Life Example

Problem: People forget to drink water throughout the day.

During Ideation:

  • One designer says: “Let’s make a water reminder app.”
  • Another says: “What if the bottle glows when it’s time to drink?”
  • Someone else adds: “Or what if it screams — ‘HYDRATE NOW!’ ”

Most ideas sound wild, but that’s the point — stretch your creativity before picking the best one.

🪄 Rules of Ideation

  • No idea is stupid. Even the weird ones can spark genius. (“A flying car” might become “a drone taxi.”)
  • Quantity over quality (at first). The more ideas you have, the better chance one will shine.
  • Build on others’ ideas. Collaboration is magic — “Yes, and…” instead of “No, but…”
  • Don’t judge or filter yet. Save criticism for later — right now, go wild!

Popular Ideation Techniques

Let’s explore two of the most powerful (and fun) ones designers love: Crazy 8s & Brainstorming.

1. Crazy 8s

A rapid sketching exercise to push your creativity. You take one sheet of paper, fold it into 8 boxes, and draw 8 ideas in 8 minutes — one per box!

  • Pick a problem (e.g., “How can we make online shopping faster?”)
  • Set a timer for 8 minutes.
  • Sketch 8 quick solutions — stick figures welcome!

Crazy 8s is like speed dating with your brain — 8 ideas in 8 minutes, and one might just be “the one”!

2. Brainstorming

The classic group idea-storm where a bunch of people throw out as many ideas as possible — the more, the better! ⚡

  • Set a clear problem statement.
  • Gather your team — designers, developers, marketers!
  • Throw out ideas (wild or serious — all count!)
  • Group and refine later.

Brainstorming is like a popcorn machine — throw in raw thoughts and great ideas start popping everywhere!

Other Cool Ideation Techniques

Technique Description Fun Twist
Mind MappingDraw a web of ideas from one central topicLike your brain throwing a party
SCAMPERSubstitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to another use, Eliminate, ReverseGreat for improving existing ideas
Worst Possible IdeaTry to come up with the dumbest idea possibleWeirdly helps you find the best one
StoryboardingDraw a sequence of how a user interacts with your ideaLike a comic strip of your solution

⚡ Why Ideation Matters

This is where creativity meets logic — and where “impossible” starts looking possible!

If you skip ideation, you might jump to the first idea and miss the one that could change everything.

“The best ideas hide behind the silly ones.”

Summary

Step Technique Goal
1️Crazy 8sGenerate lots of ideas quickly
2️BrainstormingShare and build on ideas together
3️Mind Map / SCAMPERExplore creative variations
4️StoryboardingVisualize user interactions
5️Filter & ChoosePick the most promising solutions

Ideation is like a buffet — you pile your plate with everything first, and then later decide what’s actually delicious 😋

Final Thought:
“In ideation, quantity creates quality. The more you think, the more you find. Design isn’t magic — it’s just creative problem-solving done playfully.” ✨
